College FAQs

Question

Answer

When was SGSITS established?

It was established in 1952.

Who was the first Prime Minister of India to express the desire for the institute's establishment?

Jawaharlal Nehru.

Who provided the initial donation for the institute?

Industrialist Seth Shri Kudilaji Seksaria.

Who is the institute named after?

It is named after his father, Seth Shri Govindram Seksaria, known as the 'Cotton King of India'.

What was the original name of SGSITS?

Its original name was 'Shri Govindram Seksaria Kala Bhavan'.

When was it upgraded to a degree college and what was it renamed?

In 1956, it was upgraded to a degree college and renamed 'Govindram Seksaria Technological Institute' (GSTI).

What led to the final name change to Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science?

The introduction of M.Sc. courses in Applied Sciences.

When did the institute gain autonomous status?

It was granted autonomous status in 1989 by the UGC and AICTE.

What is the motto of SGSITS?

The institute's motto is 'आचारः प्रथमो धर्मः' (Achāraḥ prathamo dharmaḥ), which translates to 'Following the right tradition is the first duty'.

How large is the SGSITS campus?

The urban campus is spread over 35 acres.

What is the address of SGSITS?

It is located at 23, Sir M. Visvesvaraya Marg, Vallabh Nagar, Indore.

What is the total building area on campus?

The total building area on campus is approximately 85,000 square meters.

How close is the campus to the Indore railway station?

The campus is within walking distance of the Indore Junction railway station.

How far is the campus from the Indore Airport?

It is about 7-8 km from Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport (Indore Airport).

Where is the campus situated within Indore?

The campus is situated in the central part of Indore.

What library facilities are available on campus?

The institute has a Central Library with a vast collection of books and digital resources.

What is the seating capacity of the Golden Jubilee Auditorium?

It has a seating capacity of 275.

What is the seating capacity of the Silver Jubilee Auditorium?

It can accommodate up to 700 people.

How many boys' hostels are on campus and what is their total capacity?

There are four boys' hostels with a total capacity of 600 students.

How many girls' hostels are on campus and what is their capacity?

There are three girls' hostels with a capacity for 467 students.

What is the Transit Hostel used for?

A Transit Hostel with 24 suites is available for research scholars and QIP candidates.

Does the campus have a guest house?

Yes, it has a guest house with a capacity to accommodate 50 people across two buildings.

What is the IDEA Lab on campus?

It is an AICTE-sponsored lab to foster innovation.

What is CIDI on campus?

It is the Centre for Innovation, Design, and Incubation.

Sports facilities include a football field, tennis court, basketball court, and cricket nets.

Yes, an indoor sports complex and a gymnasium are also available for students.

SGSITS is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal.

What is the NAAC accreditation of SGSITS?

It has been accredited with a 'NAAC A Grade'.

How many undergraduate and postgraduate courses does the institute offer?

It offers 10 undergraduate and 25 postgraduate courses.

Does the institute offer doctoral programs?

Yes, it offers Ph.D. programs in almost all engineering and science branches.
